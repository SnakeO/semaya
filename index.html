An Honest Guest Experience - Health & Safety Concerns

When I arrived at Semaya Beach Resort near Crystal Bay, the location looked promising. But problems started immediately and escalated to serious health violations.

🚨 CRITICAL: Raw Chicken Served as "Rare Beef"

FOOD SAFETY VIOLATION

Raw chicken served instead of ordered rare beef - confirmed by professional chef, AI analysis, and multiple food experts. Management lied repeatedly about what was served.

My date and I ordered rare beef for dinner. What arrived looked wrong from the start, but we only realized the horror after taking a bite. The texture and flavor were completely unlike any beef we'd ever tasted.

Restaurant Manager Andika Putra came to our table and insisted multiple times that it was beef, claiming it was just "a bad batch" - as if that made serving raw mystery meat acceptable.

Day 1: Raw meat served, manager lies about it being beef
Day 1 Evening: Severe stomach cramps begin
Day 2: Gastrointestinal illness develops, took anti-parasite medication
Day 3: Operations Manager Sindra Sindrawan initially agreed it wasn't beef, then backtracked after "talking with staff"
Kitchen Test: They cooked "same" meat - looked completely different, like actual beef

I consulted a professional chef who confirmed it definitely wasn't steak. Posted on a steak forum where dozens confirmed it wasn't beef - got BANNED because they only allow steak photos!

ChatGPT analysis: The newly cooked meat was definitely beef. My original meal couldn't be beef due to incorrect color and texture, and no reputable resort would serve chicken or pork raw.

The resort refused to pay for medical testing even though they served me raw meat.

💧 Water Issues

No complimentary water provided. When requested, charged 160,000 rupees ($10 USD) for two bottles. After complaints, they replaced sealed bottles with refillable containers - unacceptable hygiene standards for Indonesia.

Bathroom faucet produced distinctly yellow water. Hot water lasted only 15 minutes.

🚿 Zero Privacy Bathroom

Open-air toilet design with no sound privacy. When suffering from food poisoning, neighbors could hear everything. Cigarette smoke from adjacent rooms constantly wafted in due to poor design.

🚴 Safety Hazards

Rental bikes were death traps: flat tires, broken kickstands, squealing brakes that barely worked. No lights or helmets provided despite unlit roads at night.

💪 "Gym" Facilities

Laughable single unstable machine with extremely low weight capacity and plastic "dumbbells" that felt like toys.

🧹 Hygiene Violations

Housekeeping staff cleaned rooms while wearing outdoor shoes, tracking street dirt throughout our "clean" room.

✋ The Final Insult

Resort-provided wooden comb broke in half while using it. In Thai culture, this is a serious bad omen. Perfectly encapsulated the entire cursed stay.

THE REAL PROBLEM: MANAGEMENT

This is a 1-star resort as a direct result of 1-star management.

  • Restaurant Manager Andika Putra - Lied directly about food safety
  • Operations Manager Sindra Sindrawan - Backtracked under pressure
  • No senior management on-site during crisis
  • Attempted to gaslight guests about health violations
  • Zero accountability for safety standards

By the end, I couldn't wait to leave this 1-star joke of a resort. The service staff were kind, but management failures make this place completely unacceptable.
